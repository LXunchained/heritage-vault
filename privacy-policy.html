Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

ScriptVault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our automated upload tools.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to perform the automated services you request, including:

  • Authentication Data: OAuth tokens provided by TikTok to allow video uploads to your account.
  • Content Data: Videos and metadata (titles, descriptions) that you specifically choose to upload.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your account with the TikTok API.
  • Upload and publish the video content you provide.

3. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely on your local environment or server. We do not store your private credentials on our own servers. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. User Control

You can revoke ScriptVault's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account security settings.
